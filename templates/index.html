Indian Wool Farmers(A Short Glance)

1. Understanding the Indian Wool Industry

  • India is the 7th largest wool producer.
  • Major states: Rajasthan, Gujarat, J&K, HP, UP, Karnataka.
  • India mainly produces coarse wool, imports fine wool.

2. Sheep Breeds for Wool Production

Coarse Wool Breeds

Marwari, Chokla, Nali, Malpura.

Fine Wool Breeds

Gaddi, Pashmina (Himalayan regions).

3. Wool Processing Steps

  1. Shearing
  2. Sorting & Grading
  3. Washing (Scouring)
  4. Dyeing & Spinning
  5. Weaving & Knitting

4. Marketing & Government Support

Marketing Channels

Local mandis, online platforms, exports.

Government Schemes

IWDP, CWDB, National Livestock Mission.
